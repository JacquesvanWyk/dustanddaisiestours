About Us

The Road
Less Travelled

Dust & Daisies offers guided day tours and hiking trails through the breathtaking landscapes of Namaqualand. Based in Springbok, we reveal the Northern Cape's hidden treasures year-round.

Whether the veld is carpeted in wildflowers or sculpted by drought, every season tells a story worth discovering.

Section 03

Hiking Trails

Lace up and hit the trail. Four routes through untouched Namaqualand wilderness.

01

Goegap Trail

Moderate
Explore the Goegap Nature Reserve with its incredible succulent diversity. The trail winds through granite koppies and sandy plains, offering spectacular views of the Bushmanland plateau.
02

Lewerberg Trail

Challenging
A challenging ascent rewarded with panoramic views of the Spektakel Valley. Rocky terrain and indigenous flora make this a favourite for seasoned hikers.
03

Bruinkop Trail

Moderate
Follow ancient shepherds' paths across Bruinkop's rounded granite hills. Seasonal waterfalls and San rock art sites along the way.
04

Ponder Trail

Easy
A gentle, contemplative walk through fynbos and succulent gardens. Perfect for families and those seeking a peaceful communion with nature.
