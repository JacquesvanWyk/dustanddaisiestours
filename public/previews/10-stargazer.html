About Dust & Daisies

Based in Springbok in the heart of the Northern Cape, Dust & Daisies Tours offers guided day tours and hiking trails through the stunning Namaqualand landscape. Whether the veld is carpeted in wildflowers or quietly enduring drought, there is always beauty to discover.

Our experienced guides Nicky Morris and Elmarie Heyns share their deep knowledge of the region's culture, geology, and ecology.
