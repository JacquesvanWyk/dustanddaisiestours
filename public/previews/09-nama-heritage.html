About Us

Dust & Daisies Tours offers authentic Namaqualand experiences led by passionate local guides. From the famous spring wildflower carpets to the rugged copper-rich mountains, we reveal the Northern Cape's hidden treasures.

Our guides Nicky Morris and Elmarie Heyns bring decades of combined knowledge in local culture, botany, geology, and the rich Nama heritage of this ancient landscape.
