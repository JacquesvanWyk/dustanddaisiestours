About Us

Based in Springbok, the heart of Namaqualand, Dust & Daisies Tours offers guided experiences through one of South Africa's most extraordinary regions. From the legendary spring wildflower blooms to ancient geological formations, we reveal the stories hidden in this arid landscape.

Our mission is to share the raw beauty, rich culture, and natural wonders of the Northern Cape with visitors from around the world.
