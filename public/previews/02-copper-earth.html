About Us

Rooted in the Land

Born from a deep love for the Namaqualand region, Dust & Daisies offers curated experiences through one of South Africa's most extraordinary landscapes. From ancient copper mines to seasonal wildflower spectacles, every tour tells the story of this resilient land.

Our guides -- Nicky Morris and Elmarie Heyns -- bring decades of local knowledge, sharing the culture, geology, and ecology that make this place unlike any other on Earth.

Hiking Trails

Goegap Trail

Moderate

Hike through the Goegap Nature Reserve, home to desert-adapted wildlife and spectacular succulent gardens. A true Namaqualand gem.

~12km | 4-5 hours

Lewerberg Trail

Challenging

Summit the Lewerberg for panoramic views across the Bushmanland plains. Rocky terrain rewards with unforgettable vistas.

~16km | 6-7 hours

Bruinkop Trail

Moderate

A circular route through granite koppies with ancient rock art and diverse birdlife throughout.

~10km | 3-4 hours

Ponder Trail

Easy

A gentle contemplative walk through quiet veld, perfect for reflection and photography in golden hour light.

~6km | 2-3 hours
