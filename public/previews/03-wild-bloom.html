About Us

Born From a Love
of This Land

Dust & Daisies Tours was born from a deep love for Namaqualand's rugged beauty. Based in Springbok, we offer guided day tours and hiking trails that showcase the Northern Cape's spectacular landscapes, rich culture, and remarkable biodiversity.

Our guides, Nicky Morris and Elmarie Heyns, bring decades of local knowledge covering everything from wildflower ecology to the region's mining heritage and Nama culture.
