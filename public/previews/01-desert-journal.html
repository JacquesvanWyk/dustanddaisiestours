About Us

Discover Beauty in Drought
or Abundance

Dust and Daisies is based in Springbok in the heart of Namaqualand. Our guides Nicky Morris and Elmarie Heyns share their deep knowledge of culture, geology, and natural beauty with every guest who joins us on the trails.

Whether the veld is dressed in wildflowers or wearing its rugged, sun-baked face, Namaqualand never fails to inspire. We invite you to walk with us and see it through new eyes.
