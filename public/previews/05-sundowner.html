Our Story

Where the Desert Blooms

Dust and Daisies Tours invites you to experience the magic of Namaqualand with passionate local guides who know every hidden valley and secret bloom.

From the copper-rich mountains to fields of wildflowers that transform the arid landscape into a carpet of colour, every tour tells the story of resilience and beauty.
